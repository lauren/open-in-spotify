Open in Spotify

Discover songs on web radio.
Open them in Spotify.
Play them on repeat.

  1. Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:
    + Open in Spotify
  2. Go to your favorite Songza or last.fm station.
  3. Click the + Open in Spotify button to open the song you're listening to in Spotify.
  4. Play it over and over or add it to a playlist. Whatever your Spotify thing is.
